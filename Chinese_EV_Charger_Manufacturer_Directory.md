## ⚠️ Due Diligence Checklist Before Ordering

- [ ] **Request original CE test reports** — not self-declarations. Ask for the issuing lab name (TÜV, Intertek, SGS, Bureau Veritas)
- [ ] **Test OCPP connectivity** — ask for a live demo connecting their charger to an OCPP 1.6J backend
- [ ] **Verify connector type** — confirm Type 2 (IEC 62196-2), NOT GB/T
- [ ] **Check IP rating** — minimum IP54 for outdoor Bangladesh conditions
- [ ] **Order a sample unit first** — always test before bulk order
- [ ] **Clarify warranty** — who handles faulty units? Do they replace or repair?
- [ ] **Confirm export documentation** — Certificate of Origin, packing list, commercial invoice for Bangladesh customs (HS 8504.40)
