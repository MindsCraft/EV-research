01 / Executive Summary

The Opportunity

Capturing first-mover advantage in Bangladesh's emerging EV ecosystem.

Market Inflection

Runner Automobiles signed a BYD local assembly deal in early 2026, signaling a major surge in EV volume. National targets demand 15-30% EV adoption by 2030.

Policy Tailwind

The FY 2026-27 budget eliminated all import duties on EV chargers (down from 39.75%), creating a temporary cost window for early infrastructure deployment.

Severe Deficit

Fewer than 20 functional public charging stations exist today. The government has set a national target of 1,200 public stations.

Core Strategic Approach

We will operate as a full-service Charge Point Operator (CPO). We will import hardware from Chinese suppliers at 0% duty, secure premium urban site agreements, and deploy a proprietary OCPP software platform to manage billing, remote monitoring, and driver experience.

03 / Global Market Lessons

What Other Countries Learned

Applying proven international insights to the Bangladesh rollout.

🇮🇳 India Reliability Phase

Crisis

Over-deployment of low-quality chargers led to a 25%+ non-functional rate, damaging driver trust.

Our Action

Ensure remote OCPP diagnostics and a 24hr maintenance SLA from Day 1.

🇻🇳 Vietnam Market Capture

Pattern

VinFast locked the market by deploying 150,000 ports early. Closed network limits multi-car growth.

Our Action

Build an OCPP-open network accepting all EV brands to capture maximum utilization.

🇨🇳 China Margin Maturity

Pattern

Margin wars showed hardware commoditizes. Software platforms and brand lock-in are the real moats.

Our Action

White-label premium hardware with our brand to establish local presence.

🇱🇰 Sri Lanka Regulatory Tightening

Pattern

Informal CPOs faced sudden closures as the state utility PUCSL standardizes safety codes.

Our Action

Build SREDA-compliant infrastructure from Day 1 to avoid regulatory risks.

04 / Deployment Strategy

Phase 1 Target Segments

Securing premium location segments with high utilization potential.

Private & Corporate AC

  • Private Homes: AC 7.4-22kW wallboxes for overnight garage charging.
  • Office Campuses: employee charging during office hours.
  • Hotels: Premium amenity differentiator for overnight guests.

Public DC Fast Charging

  • Shopping Malls: DC 30-60kW chargers matching average 2-3 hour dwell times.
  • City Hubs: DC 60-120kW high-turnover hubs in commercial nodes.
  • Destination Parking: High density Gulshan / Banani lots.

Phase 1 Exclusions

Large vehicle depots and long-distance highway corridors are excluded from Phase 1. These require massive grid reinforcement that is not supported by current passenger EV adoption volumes. We will enter this space in Year 3.
