---

## Key Selection Criteria

All 7 suppliers below were selected based on:
- ✅ OCPP 1.6J compliant (minimum) — must work with any CPMS software
- ✅ No forced proprietary software lock-in
- ✅ CCS2 / Type 2 connector support (Bangladesh-compatible)
- ✅ CE or equivalent international safety certification
- ✅ Willing to sell spare parts separately (not full-unit returns)
- ✅ Demonstrated export track record to 50+ countries
- ✅ IP54 or higher ingress rating (monsoon + heat resilience)

## Before Contacting Any Supplier — Checklist

1. **Verify certificates independently** — TÜV at certipedia.com, CE via EU NANDO database, OCPP listing at openchargealliance.org
2. **Request OCPP test connection** before placing any order — confirm charger talks to your CPMS
3. **Ask about spare parts supply** — confirm individual modules/parts can be purchased separately (not full-unit returns)
4. **Order 2–3 sample units first** — test in Bangladesh conditions before bulk order
5. **For orders above $25,000** — commission a factory audit (SGS, TÜV, or Bureau Veritas: ~$500–1,500)
